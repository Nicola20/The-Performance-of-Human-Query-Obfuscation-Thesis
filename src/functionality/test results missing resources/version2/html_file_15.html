Services > Hazardous Materials

Please do not discard of any household or commercial hazardous materials in your trash, recycling or yard waste containers. It is dangerous for BFI to transport hazardous materials and illegal to dispose of them in California landfills.

Instead bring your hazardous materials to the Salinas Valley Solid Waste Authorityâs household hazardous materials facility at 139 Sun Street in Salinas.

Some examples of materials classified as hazardous are as follows:

  • Cleaning supplies
  • Chemicals
  • Batteries
  • Freon (as found in refrigerators and freezers)
  • Latex Paint

Televisions and Computer appliances can be taken to any of the county landfills or on Sun Street..

For more information on hazardous materials, hours of operations and directions please call 831-758-7928.
