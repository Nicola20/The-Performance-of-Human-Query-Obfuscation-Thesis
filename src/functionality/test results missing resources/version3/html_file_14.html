Good Nutrition for People with HIV/AIDS
Lange Video
Lange Video Productions
Healthcare

This video explains the role good nutrition I plays for people with HIV/AIDS and offers specifics as to how much and what a person with- HIV/AIDS should eat and drink. The key point emphasized is that a person with HIV/AIDS needs extra fluids, calories, proteins, and vitamins and minerals because the infection increases metabolic rate and the body needs more energy to fight the infection. The presentation provides realistic suggestions on how someone with a decreased appetite can take in sufficient nutrients and also emphasizes that food must be fresh and clean to decrease the risk of taxing the body's already stressed immune system.
